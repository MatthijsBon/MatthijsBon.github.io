About Matthijs Bon

My Interests

Geomatics

The field of geomatics encompasses everything that relates to data about a location. This can vary from data acquisition, data analysis to data visualization. This is usually accompanied with programming. I enjoy geomatics since it has in my opinion an essential role in making society and economy a more sustainable place.

Data Visualization

One specific aspect I enjoy in geomatics is data visualization. Using D3.js, one is able to visualize almost everything, including geographic information in an easy and comprehensible way. I think being able to visualize complex data is very important, since it conveys the message to other people, including those not familiar with your work.

Sports

In my free time I enjoy playing sports. In winter season I like to spend as much time as possible in the mountain with snowboarding. In the meantime I play volleybal and recently I have started running. I like how sports keeps me focussed and it can easily distract me from worrying about everyday things.
